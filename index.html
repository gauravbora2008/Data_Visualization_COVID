    What is COVID-19?

    This is a new virus called the severe acute respiratory syndrome. In March 2020, the World Health Organization(WHO) declared COVID-19 a pandemic.

    How does COVID-19 spread?

    It spreads from person to person among those in close contact(within about 6 feet). It spreads via respiratory droplets infected with the virus.

    Can COVID-19 be prevented or treated?

    There is no vaccine currently available for the coronavirus disease. Treatment is directed at relieving symptoms.

      The then-mystery illness, Covid-19 was first reported in Wuhan last December, not long before the lead up to the Lunar New Year when China undertakes the world’s largest mass migration.
